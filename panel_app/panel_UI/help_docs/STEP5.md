## Review and Launch

This is your summary and final confirmation step.

- Please review your selected region, variables, and any climate indices below.
- When you are ready, click **Launch** to submit your job.

**What happens next?**

- Your requested downscaling and/or climate index calculations will be run automatically.
- When processing is complete, **a download link for each output will be emailed to you** at the address connected to your account.
- You do **not** need to keep this page open; you can close your browser or navigate away.

> **Reminder:**  
> **Note:** All output files are temporary and will be **deleted after 7 days**.  
> Please download your files promptly from the emailed links.
